Questions, answered

Before you decide

What if I don't know which course I need?
That's exactly what the advisor is for. Describe your situation at the top of the page and it'll recommend the right program, and explain why, before you spend a dollar.
Is Prosci certification actually worth it?
Prosci is the most widely recognized change-management certification, used by 80%+ of the Fortune 100. You leave with the ADKAR methodology, a reusable toolkit, and a plan applied to a live project, not just a certificate.
What's the difference between virtual and in-person?
Both cover identical content and earn the same certification. Virtual cohorts are live and instructor-led, ideal for distributed teams; in-person adds hands-on collaboration and networking. The advisor can recommend based on your timeline.
How much time will this take?
It ranges from a few hours of self-paced eLearning to a half-day manager workshop to a 3-day practitioner certification. You'll see the exact commitment on every recommendation before enrolling.
Do you offer team or enterprise pricing?
Yes. Tell the advisor you're enrolling a group or rolling out org-wide and it'll route you to team workshops and enterprise licensing, with a dedicated Prosci advisor for volume pricing.

Still not sure where to start?

Tell the advisor what you're facing in a sentence. You'll get a clear, personalized path in seconds, no commitment required.
